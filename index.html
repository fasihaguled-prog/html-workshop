My love for spring

Spring is my favorite time of the year. The birds return and fill the air with their singing, and the asphalt finally appears as the snow melts away. Walking feels light and easy again, and the sun brings warmth and energy. Everything looks fresh and clean, especially the soft green color in the grass and trees. The first flowers make the season feel alive and beautiful.
